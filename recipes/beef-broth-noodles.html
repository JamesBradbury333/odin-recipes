Beef Broth Noodles

Description

Homemade hand-pulled noodles made with water, flour, and oil are cooked until tender and springy, and served with a delicious and aromatic homemade beef broth, daikon, garlic, and chili oil for a truly special Chinese soup from Lanzhou in Northwest China.

Ingredients

Soup

  • 1 gallon of water
  • 2 pounds of bonless beef shoulder, cubed
  • 1/4 cup of sliced fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons of Szechuan peppercorns
  • 2 dried tsaoko
  • 2 pieces of dried mandarin orange peel
  • 4 bay leaves
  • salt to taste

Noodles

  • 8 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cups of vegetable oil

To Garnish

  • 1 pound of daikon, peeled and sliced
  • 2 stalks of freen garlic, chopped
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 8 drops of chili oil, or to taste
